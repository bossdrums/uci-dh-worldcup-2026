How to Watch in the UK

In the UK and Ireland, every UCI Downhill World Cup round is broadcast live and on demand through Warner Bros. Discovery's platforms:

TNT Sports
Live TV broadcast
HBO Max
Live streaming
discovery+
Streaming (TNT tier)

UK Pricing

To watch live in the UK you will need a TNT Sports subscription, which costs approximately £25.99/month (or £30.99 on a rolling monthly plan).

Free Options

Free race highlights are available after each event on the UCI MTB World Series YouTube channel. Red Bull TV also features highlight packages.
